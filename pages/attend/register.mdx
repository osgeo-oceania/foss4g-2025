## Registration

Registration includes conference registration to presentations and talks, morning tea, lunch and afternoon tea for Wednesday 19th, Thursday 20th and Friday 21st November.

It also includes access to the Icebreaker on Tuesday 18th November and the Community Events and Code Sprints on Saturday 22nd and Sunday 23rd November.

| Type                  | Details                                                                                                                   | Cost (NZD) |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| Early Bird            | Early bird registration, available until 28th June.                                                                       | $700       |
| Standard              | General registration, available from 28th June until 20th September.                                                      | $950       |
| Last Chance           | Last minute registration after 20th September.                                                                            | $1500      |
| True Believer         | Also includes a dinner ticket and a contribution to the Travel Grant Program.                                             | $1950      |
| Student/Retired       | For secondary and tertiary students at any institution, and for people who are no longer in paid work.                    | $500       |
| Community Contributor | For open source, open data or volunteer community contributors otherwise not supported by another organisation to attend. | $500       |

Please note our [Privacy Policy.](/attend/privacy-policy)
